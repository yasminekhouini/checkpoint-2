career:

  • Born on May 1, 2004, Charli D'Amelio is an American social media personality and dancer.
  • Her career began on TikTok in the summer of 2019, uploading dance videos to trending songs on the platform.
  • Her first TikTok was a lip-syncing video with her friend. She now has over 71.9 million TikTok followers, 23.9 million Instagram followers, 5.58 million YouTube subscribers, and 3.3 million Twitter followers.
  • She became the most-followed account on TikTok on March 25, 2020.
  • In 2019, former Sony Music executive Barbara Jones signed D’Amelio to her management company, Outshine Talent.
  • In January 2020, D'Amelio signed with talent agency UTA.
  • She appeared in a Super Bowl commercial for Sabra Hummus along with other celebrities.
  • She was invited to attend Super Bowl LIV and meet Jennifer Lopez to create the TikTok challenge "J Lo Super Bowl Challenge."
  • In January 2020, Charli was labeled TikTok's "reigning queen" by the New York Times.

charli's personal life:

Her immediate family includes politician and father, Marc D'Amelio, her mother, Heidi D'Amelio, and her sister, Dixie D’Amelio.
D'Amelio dated fellow TikTok star Chase Hudson, in which they confirmed their relationship in January of 2020. On April 14 of the same year, she and Chase announced their split.
